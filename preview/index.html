About me

Connection has always been my language

I’m Jennifer, a Daisy Award–winning nurse leader whose work spans Critical Care, ICU leadership, and nursing professional development. I help people stay human in the work — with professionalism, authenticity, and unfiltered honesty.

As founder and host of The Call Light Collective, I champion healthcare storytelling and culture. Whether on a stage, a podcast, or a panel, the goal is the same: spaces where people feel seen and supported.

I work with collaborators, sponsors, and audiences who care about nursing culture, healing-centered stories, and voices that tell the truth with heart.
